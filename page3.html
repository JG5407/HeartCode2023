Some ways to combat trolling and prevent incidents of being trolled:

  1. Ignore the trolls
  2. Talk to someone you trust
  3. Block/delete the comment
  4. Think twice about replying
  5. If you do reply, stand up for yourself
  6. Stay away from the platform
  7. Log off
