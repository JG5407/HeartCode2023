Definition of trolling:

The term “troll” usually refers to someone who maliciously harasses or cyberbullies other people. They upload unnecessary comments on social media posts, that can be either offensive content or racist, homophobic, misogynistic, or hateful comments.

Some facts about trolling in 2023:

  • Instagram is the social media site where most young people report experiencing trolling, with 42% of those surveyed experiencing harassment on the platform.
  • 60% of young people have witnessed online trolling. Most do not intervene.
  • Only 1 in 10 teen victims will inform a parent or trusted adult of their experiences of being trolled.
  • 19.2% of students had stayed home from school because of trolling.
  • About 28% of children around the world have experienced trolling that is racially motivated, with the highest rates occurring in India, South Africa and the U.S.
